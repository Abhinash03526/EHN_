About Us

‘Economics Hub Nepal’ is a social and online group that works to educate people in Nepal from all walks of life about economics and finance. The organization's main goal is to serve as a central location where economic enthusiasts, students, and professionals can learn about, discuss, and exchange the most recent economic trends and policies.
